Slack
08/2013
Slack is a cloud-based of proprietary team collaboration tools and services, founded by Stewart Butterfield. Slack started out as an internal tool used by his company, Tiny Speck, in the development of Glitch, a now-defunct online game.
Whatsapp
2009
WhatsApp is a cross-platform instant messaging and voice calling application for smartphones. In addition to text messaging, users can send images, videos, and PDF documents, besides you can make free calls over an Internet connection.
Telegram
14/08/2013
Telegram is a cloud-based instant messaging service. Telegram is available for smartphones or tablets, computers, and also as a web application. Users can send messages and exchange photos, videos, stickers and files of any kind.
Trello
2011
Trello is a web-based project management application originally created by Fog Creek Software in 2011. In 2014 it became a company. It operates a Freemium business model, in which the free or paid version is made available to the user.
Skype
08/2003
Skype is a software that allows communication over the Internet through voice and video connections, created by Janus Friis and Niklas Zennstrom. Skype launched in 2003. In 2005 it was sold to eBay and has been owned by Microsoft since May 2011.
